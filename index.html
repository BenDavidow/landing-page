The Future Of Forecasting

Augur is a decentralized prediciton market platform built on Ethereum.
Augur combines prediciton markets with the wisdom of the crowd to create a stunningly accurate forcasting tool.

   
What is Augur?



Get rewarded for your knowledge and insight.

Augur is a prediction market platform that rewards you for correctly predicting real world events.

Predict The Outcome

You make your predictions by trading virtual shares in the outcome of events happening in the real world. If you think Hilary Clinton will be elected President, you'll buy shares in that outcome. The prices of all shares in a market adds up to one dollar, so if you buy a share at even odds, it will cost you 50 cents. If you're correct, you'll get back one dollar for every share you purchased. If you're wrong, then you'll lose the money used to purchase your shares. As markets get liquidity, the price they trade at represents a percentage prediction of such outcome occurring.

Report The Outcome

Augur is a decentralized prediction market platform, meaning we have no centralized source to resolve markets. We leverage the wisdom of the crowd, by asking all Reputation token holders to log onto Augur once every two months to report on the outcomes of markets. You stake your Reputation tokens, and if you report within consensus, you will receive a share of the market fees for the reporting period. If you report out of consensus, the Reputation you used to stake your reports will be distributed to reporters who reported within consensus. Learn about REP here.

Augur's Edge: Decentralization

No Central Servers

Augur is decentralized and runs on the Ethereum blockchain, meaning there is no central point of failure or control.

Create Your Own Markets

Users can use Augur to create any prediction market. There are no rules governing the types of markets users can create.

Lower Fees

When it's time to get paid out, users will endure a lower cost for settleing their position compared to Betfair or PredictIt.

Distributed Reporting

The wisdom of the crowd is used to resolve markets. Reputation holders are financially incentivized to report accurately.

Automated Payouts

Trustless, automated and transparent payouts are now possible. When your position settles, Augurs contracts pay you immediatley.

No Limits

There is no deposit or bet limit. Augur does not care who you are or where you're located as long as you can run the software.
